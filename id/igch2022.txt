    <span rel="mint" resource="bruttii">Brettii: 4 AE</span>
         <span rel="mint" resource="carthage">Carthage: 103 quarter-shekels (102 female

    Disposition: Reggio, 103 AR
    Ferri, NSc 1926, p. 329 
    Pfeiler, JNG 1964, pp. 34-6
    The quarter-shekel with male hd. is de-
        scribed by Ferri as being a negro hd. 
        The coin is, however, form the issue 
        ascribed to Hannibal in Spain (Essays 
        Mattingly, pl. 3, 7n); it weighs 1.74 gm., 
        and is more worn than the majority 
        of quarter-shekels in this hd.

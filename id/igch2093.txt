    Disposition: dispersed
    Sambon, F.P.L. 248 (1885), p. 20; 206 (1903), p. 11
    Jenkins, Gela, pp. 148-9
    Of two AV of Acragas at Syracuse (M. T. 
        Currò Pisanò, AIIN 1962-4, p. 221, 
        s.v. Sortino, before 1889), one has a
        hd. provenance arid is probably from 
        this find. The other may be a stray 
        find from Sortino (about 30 km. NW 
        of Syracuse), unless indeed this hd. 
        was actually found at Sortino and only 
        marketed at Catania. Jenkins notes the 
        existence in private possession in 
        England of 1 Acragas, 1 KA, and 4 
        Syracuse, which may also derive from 
        this hd.

 <div property="skos:prefLabel" xml:lang="en">Noricum</div>
 <div property="skos:definition" xml:lang="en">Noricum, within the context of ancient history, is the Latin name for a Celtic kingdom, or federation of twelve tribes, including most of modern Austria and part of Slovenia. It became a province of the Roman Empire.</div>

    Disposition: Ankara 2003 (inv. no. 1485) 
    Information from Saadet Taner
    The issues of Amisus are by far the most 
        numerous, followed by those of Sinope 
        and Amastris. The numbers of coins 
        from the other mints are small (Ga-
        ziura 2; Pimolisa 1). It is possible 
        that the lot in Ankara derives from 
        the large hd. mentioned in JNG 1969, 
        p. 79, as appearing already in 1947 at 
        Ordu
    Information from P. Franke indicates that 
        there should probably be added to 
        this hd. a few AE from Pergamum, 
        Rhodes, Side, Laodiceia ad Mare, and 
        Seleuceia Pieria

      <pre>1798 Atrek valley, W of Bujnurd (anc. 
    district of Hyrcania), c. 1965
    Burial: c. 209 B.C.
    Contents: c. 1600 AR
    Alexander III: c. 600 lifetime and early 
        post. dr.
            1 post. tetradr.
    Philip III: 15+ dr.
    Lysimachus: 5+ dr.
    Antiochus II: 4+ dr. (Bactra 1+, unc. 
        mints 3+)
    Arsaces I, Parthia: c. 60 dr.
    Arsaces II, Parthia: c. 850 dr.
    Diodotus, Bactria: 1+ dr.
    Disposition: dispersed
    Abgarians-Sellwood, NC 1971, pp. 103-119, illus.
    The attribution of drachms to Arsaces I 
        and II is still doubtful. Some scholars 
        prefer an attribution to Mithradates I, 
        in which case the date of burial must 
        be lowered to c. 150 B.C.
</pre>

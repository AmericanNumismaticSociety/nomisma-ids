            1 <span rel="mint" resource="larissa">Larissa imit.</span>
    Disposition: Negotin
    Vucković-Todorović, Starinar 20 (1969), pp. 391-
        403, illus.
    The author considers this and no. 458 
        below (Noe 959) to be portions of the 
        same hd. from Jabukovac. If so, how-
        ever, his burial date of c. 280 B.C. fails 
        to take into account a coin of Attalus I 
        which provides the basis for Hunter's 
        burial date of c. 220 for Noe 959. 
        Moreover, the provenance of Noe 959 
        is now considered to be Zemun, near 
        Belgrade, and not south Serbia

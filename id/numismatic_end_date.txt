   <div property="skos:prefLabel" xml:lang="en">Numismatic End Date</div>
   <div property="skos:definition" xml:lang="en">The date at which an entity ceased to issue coins or to be responsible for the issuance of coins.</div>
<div property="rdfs:comment" xml:lang="en">Nomisma dates conform to ISO 8601:2000 2nd Edition, which specifies that '0000' is the value for 1BC.</div></div>

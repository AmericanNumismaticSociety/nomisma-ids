<pre>A hoard of third century antoniniani numbering about 47,000 was discovered near Normanby, Lincolnshire, England in December of 1985. From 1985-1987, the hoard was processed by The British Museum under the direction of Roger Bland.
    Disposition: Coins can be found in The British Museum and University of Virginia Art Museum</pre>

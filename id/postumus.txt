   <div property="skos:definition" xml:lang="en">Marcus Cassianius Latinius Postumus[2] was a Roman emperor of Batavian origin. He usurped power from Gallienus in 260 and formed the so-called Gallic Empire. He was recognised in Gaul, Germania, Britannia and Iberia until his murder in 269.</div>
